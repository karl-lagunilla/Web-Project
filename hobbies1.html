Being active!

I was always active in high school but I didn't really care about building strength. I was a freshmen in college and my frined introduced me to resistance training. I was able to gain a lot of muscle weight.

From being 150 after high school, now I'm 165 in weight. That's a good 15 pounds of muscle mass.

Lifting has taught me a lot. From injuries, different training regimes, and getting to know how the mind-muscle connection works. This hobby ended up becoming a lifestyle for me. I'm trying to hit many different lifting goals in life.
